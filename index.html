Paper (ICRA 2025) Code

This page uses a QR code as the tracking image. When the camera sees that printed target, the video appears on top of it in the browser.

Camera Stage

The AR scene will appear here after the image target is compiled and the camera starts.

Point your camera at the printed QR code.

How To Use It

Preparing the AR target.

  1. Tap Start AR and allow camera access.
  2. Point the camera at the QR code.
  3. The video will begin from frame one and stay attached to the image while it remains visible.
